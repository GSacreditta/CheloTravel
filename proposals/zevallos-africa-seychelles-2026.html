Nairobi Sightseeing
Full day exploring Nairobi. All entrance fees and lunch included.

Fly by light aircraft to the Chyulu Hills. 3 nights in a Deluxe Pool Suite. Private plunge pool, rooftop star bed, Kilimanjaro views. All meals, game drives, and activities included.

Mara Plains Camp · Masai Mara
Fly via Nairobi to the Olare Motorogi Conservancy. 3 nights in a Camp Suite. Canvas tents on elevated wooden decks overlooking the savanna. All meals and game viewing included.
May 16
Nairobi · Airport Overnight
