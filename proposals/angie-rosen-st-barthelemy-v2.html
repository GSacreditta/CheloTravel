The Villa

St-Jean, St. Barthélemy

A modern luxury villa perched above St-Jean with heated infinity pool, panoramic bay views, and five spacious bedrooms. Your private home base for the week — complete with a gourmet kitchen and outdoor BBQ for the farewell dinner.

From Italian fine dining in Gustavia to beachfront extravaganzas and a cabaret evening at Pointe Milou. Every lunch and dinner hand-picked at St. Barth’s most coveted tables — ending with a private BBQ sunset at the villa.

Itinerary

Day by Day

Wednesday, May 6
Arrival in St. Barthélemy
Arrive on the island and settle into Villa Senna in St-Jean. Unwind by the infinity pool with views over the bay.
May 6 · 7:30 PM
Dinner — L’Isola
Sophisticated Italian dining in the heart of Gustavia. Artisanal pasta, premium seafood, and an extensive Italian wine cellar on Rue du Roi Oscar II.
Thursday, May 7 · 11:00 AM
Beach & Lunch — Cheval Blanc St-Barth Isle de France
Morning on the pristine sands of Flamands Bay at Cheval Blanc. Lunch at 12:30 PM beachside — one of the island’s most beautiful settings with turquoise waters and LVMH hospitality.
May 7 · 7:30 PM
Dinner — Le Toiny Beach Extravaganza
The legendary Thursday night Beach Extravaganza at Le Toiny. Gourmet cuisine, live music, and fire shows on one of the island’s most untamed beaches at Anse de Toiny.
Friday, May 8 · 2:00 PM
Lunch — NAO Beach Club
Contemporary Japanese-Mediterranean fusion on Saint-Jean Bay. Daybeds, cabanas, and afternoon DJ sets with turquoise water views at this chic beach club.
May 8 · 9:00 PM
Dinner — Sella
Modern Israeli-Mediterranean cuisine by Michelin-starred Chef Assaf Granit. Candlelit atmosphere with sunset harbor views in Gustavia. DJ entertainment into the night.
Saturday, May 9 · 2:00 PM
Lunch — Le Barthélemy
Ultra-luxury beachside dining at Grand Cul-de-Sac. Parisian elegance meets Caribbean warmth at this Condé Nast award-winning hotel overlooking the turquoise double-aspect bay.
May 9 · 8:30 PM
Dinner — Le Ti St Barth
An iconic St. Barth’s experience — fine dining meets cabaret at Pointe Milou. Nightly international shows, a costume corner, and VIP nightclub atmosphere. Currently featuring “Kingdom.”
Sunday, May 10 · 2:30 PM
Lunch — La Guérite
Mediterranean cuisine by Chef Yiannis Kioroglou with a focus on seasonal products. Harbor views, live music, and the relaxed glamour of a Sunday afternoon in Gustavia.
May 10 · Evening
Farewell BBQ — Villa Senna
A private farewell dinner at the villa. Fire up the outdoor BBQ, open the wine, and enjoy sunset over the bay from your infinity pool terrace. The perfect way to close the trip.
Monday, May 11
Return Home
Depart St. Barthélemy. Until next time.
